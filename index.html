Privacy Policy for SketchMagic

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Overview

SketchMagic ("we", "our", or "the app") is a drawing transformation app that uses AI to transform children's drawings into animated, cartoon-style images. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Images You Provide

  • What: Photos you take or select from your library
  • Why: To transform your drawings using AI
  • How it works: Images are securely processed using AI technology, then the transformed result is returned to your device
  • Storage: Images are processed but not permanently stored. Transformed images are saved locally on your device only.

Purchase Information

  • What: Records of in-app purchases (credits, subscriptions)
  • Why: To provide you with the credits you purchased
  • Storage: Purchase records are stored securely on your device and managed by Apple's App Store

Device Information

  • What: Basic device type for compatibility
  • Why: To ensure the app works correctly on your device
  • Storage: Not stored or transmitted

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Browsing history
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers

Third-Party Services

AI Processing

We use third-party AI services to transform your drawings. When you submit an image:

  • The image is securely transmitted for AI processing
  • The AI processes the image and returns the transformed version
  • Your images are not used as training data
  • Images are not stored after processing

Apple App Store

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. Apple's handling of payment information is governed by their Privacy Policy.

Data Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Purchase data is stored using Apple's secure storage mechanisms
  • We do not maintain user accounts or databases of personal information
  • Images are processed but not permanently stored

Children's Privacy

SketchMagic is designed to be family-friendly. We:

  • Do not collect personal information from children
  • Do not require account creation
  • Do not include advertising
  • Do not include social features

Parents can manage purchases through iOS Screen Time settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your locally stored data (credits, saved artwork in the app)
  • Delete transformed images: Remove saved images from within the app or by deleting the app
  • Opt-out: Choose not to use the transformation feature

Important Note About Purchase Data

Your credit balance is stored securely on your device using Apple's recommended practices. This data may persist after app deletion to prevent loss of purchased credits. To fully remove this data, you may need to reset your device or contact us for assistance.
